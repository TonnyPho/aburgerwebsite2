Burgers

Welcome to the page that contains information about burgers, the origin, and a list of burgers

List:

1.) McDonalds Grand Mac

The grand mac was a limited edition version of the original Mcdonalds Big Mac, it was a larger version of the big mac for the customers that didn't believe the orginal was enough

2.) Popeyes Chicken Sandwich
The popeyes chicken sandwich was just recently released and was immediately a success due to the very positive feedback of others. It began selling out in many popeyes across the globe just days after it was released
3.) In-N-Out Double Double

The In-N-Out's double double is known to be a classic, it consists of fresh lettuce, tomatoes, raw onions, thousand island spread, and beef patties.
